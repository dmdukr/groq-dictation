Audio Processing
Noise suppression (RNNoise)
Removes background noise — keyboard typing, fan hum, air conditioning. Recommended on for all environments. Uses a small neural network, no noticeable CPU impact.

Automatically adjusts your microphone volume so you're always heard clearly — whether you speak quietly or loudly, close or far from the mic. The target level below controls how loud the output should be.

Voice detection sensitivity
Controls how aggressively the app detects when you stop talking. Lower = more sensitive, catches soft speech but may include background noise. Higher = stricter, cleanly cuts silence but may trim quiet endings. Level 2 works well for most people.
